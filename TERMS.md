Last updated: 2026-03-18

By using KeySpeak you agree to the following terms.

## 1. What KeySpeak Does

KeySpeak is a local speech-to-text application for macOS. It records audio from your microphone, transcribes it on your device using a local AI model, and types the result into the active application.

## 2. Privacy and Data

- All audio is processed entirely on your Mac.
- No audio, transcriptions, or personal data are transmitted to any server.
- No analytics or telemetry are collected.
- Audio is held in memory only during recording and discarded after transcription.

## 3. Microphone Access

KeySpeak requires microphone access to function. You can revoke this at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone.

## 4. Accessibility Access

KeySpeak requires Accessibility access to register global hotkeys and type text into other applications. You can revoke this at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility.
